MOON SIGHTING UPDATE

‎السلام عليكم ورحمة الله وبركاته

JUMADA-AL-AWWAL 1446

*Moonsighting update*

Assalamu-Alaikum

*MOON  WAS SIGHTED*

Respected Ulamaa,Brothers and Sisters,

The crescent moon to commence the month of Jumada-al-Awwal 1446 AH was looked for after the sunset on Saturday 02nd November 2024. Reports from committees confirm that the moon was sighted. THE HILAL COMMITTEE OF TORONTO & VICINITY has accepted these reports and has declared that the month of Rabi-al-Thani 1446 AH will complete 29 days. therefore, the 1st day of Jumada-al-Awwal 1446 AH will be on Sunday November 3rd, 2024. Correspondingly the Ashurah 10th Muharram will be Tuesday July 16th 2024.
